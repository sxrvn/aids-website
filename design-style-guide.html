01 · Design Philosophy
Aesthetic Direction
Dark Tech Glassmorphism
Vibe
AWS re:Invent meets Cyberpunk Dashboard
Tone
Premium · Techy · High Contrast
Personality
Confident · Clean · Dark-native

Core Principles
· Everything lives on a deep navy/charcoal background — never white, never grey
· Depth is created through layered blur + opacity not solid fills
· Color is used for meaning and category not decoration
· Glows and borders are always semi-transparent, never solid
· Hierarchy is built through opacity and weight, not size alone
· No emojis — use clean SVG line icons only

10 · Do's & Don'ts
Do
· Use deep navy base (#030d1f)
· Layer orbs with blur(80px)
· Semi-transparent borders only
· SVG line icons, stroke-width 1.8
· Gradient text for hero headings
· letter-spacing on all labels
· backdrop-filter on cards
· Animate with pulse / fade only
· Use color to encode meaning
Don't
· No white or grey backgrounds
· No solid opaque borders
· No emojis in UI elements
· No more than 5 accent colors
· No flat non-blurred cards
· No pixel fonts or serif
· No bold text as decoration
· No pure black (#000) anywhere
· No more than 3 orbs per screen
